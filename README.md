# Time-has-run-out
The sad story behind my oc

Losing someone is like losing the meaning of life, no one knows when they will be gone forever...
That's what happened to Kevin (Main oc)
-
📄Last message 
"Kevin, lately, mom and dad have been very busy working, we have a lot of tasks to do. Son, take good care of yourself there, maybe you won't miss us both because we are working.But, we miss you so much son...We really love you." 

Kevin stared at the letter with a sad expression, as he couldn't see his parents. He then put the letter on the table and said to himself, "Why? Why won't they come home? I've sent them several letters...I...I miss them so much." Then he lay on the bed confused, he was so bored.


- 5 Days Later...
Kevin woke up to a letter and a box with unknown contents. He opened the letter and suddenly froze... his shocked expression turning into deep sadness. "It's impossible...this is impossible, how could-" He said, he was silent, his tears started to fall slowly but they were getting heavier like ice melting because of the pressure.
The letter contained his parents' last message before being killed by enemy troops. Her heart was shattered, feeling like it had been stabbed repeatedly with knives. She hadn't expected this news. She opened the box, which turned out to contain her birthday present from yesterday.
She started crying even harder because she knew the gift was her favorite winter jacket. She wanted to wear it with her parents, but before that could happen...They had already left him.
